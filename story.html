ðŸ’—Noodle was born in Osaka, Kansai, Japan on October 31, 1990. She spent a portion of her childhood in Japan as a subject of a classified Japanese super soldier project under the management of the Japanese scientist, Mr. Kyuzo.

ðŸ’—Noodle and twenty-two other children were trained for the sole purpose of fighting as soldiers for the Japanese military and government.
